Privacy Policy — CookSwipe

Effective date: June 13, 2026

CookSwipe ("we", "our", or "us") is a meal decision app for Android. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

Anonymous Identifier

When you first open CookSwipe, we assign you a random anonymous ID using Firebase Anonymous Authentication. This ID is not linked to your name, email, phone number, or any personally identifiable information.

Usage Data

We collect anonymised usage data via Firebase Analytics, including:

  • Meal categories browsed
  • Number of swipes per session
  • App open/close events
  • Device type and OS version (anonymised)

This data is used solely to improve app performance and user experience. It cannot be used to identify you personally.

Preferences You Set

Your food preferences (vegetarian/non-vegetarian, region, cooking time) are stored locally on your device and optionally synced to Firebase Firestore under your anonymous ID.

2. Advertising

CookSwipe uses Google AdMob to display interstitial ads (maximum one per day). AdMob may use your device's advertising ID to show relevant ads. You can reset or opt out of personalised ads in your device settings under Google → Ads.

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Your name, email address, or phone number
  • Your location
  • Your contacts or photos
  • Any payment information

4. Third-Party Services

CookSwipe uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase (Google) — anonymous auth, database, analytics, crash reporting
  • Google AdMob — advertising

5. Data Retention

Anonymous usage data is retained for up to 14 months as per Firebase Analytics defaults. You can request deletion of your anonymous data by contacting us at the email below.

6. Children's Privacy

CookSwipe is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
